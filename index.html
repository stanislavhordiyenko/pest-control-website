Our Process

1. Initial Inspection

2. Customized Treatment Plan

Based on the inspection, we create a tailored treatment plan, including eco-friendly options. We prioritize solutions that are safe for children, pets, and the environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does pest control cost in Sydney?

Our residential pest control services have fixed pricing: $199 for single-story homes and $249 for double-story homes (properties up to 700m² land size). This includes complete internal and external treatment with no hidden fees.

Are your pest control products safe for children and pets?

Yes, absolutely. We use only eco-friendly, approved products that are safe for human health and pets. All our chemicals meet Australian safety standards and are applied by licensed technicians following strict safety protocols. We recommend keeping pets and children away during treatment and for 2-4 hours after, allowing products to dry completely.

How long does a pest control treatment take?

A standard residential pest control treatment typically takes 1-2 hours depending on the size of your property and the severity of the infestation. We conduct a thorough inspection, treat all affected areas both internally and externally, and provide you with detailed aftercare instructions.

How often should I get pest control done?

For optimal protection, we recommend quarterly pest control treatments (every 3 months). However, this can vary based on your property's pest pressure, location, and previous pest history. We also offer semi-annual (6 monthly) and annual treatment plans. Our technicians will advise the best schedule for your specific situation.

Do I need to prepare my home before pest control treatment?

Minimal preparation is required. We ask that you clear kitchen benches, move items away from walls where possible, and ensure access to all rooms including roof spaces. Remove or cover pet food and water bowls. Our technicians will provide specific instructions when booking your service.

How quickly can you come out for pest control?

We offer same-day service in many cases, depending on availability and location. Emergency pest control services are available for urgent situations. Simply call us on 0413 818 626 or send an SMS to book, and we'll schedule the earliest available appointment.

What areas do you service?

We service all of South Western Sydney and surrounding suburbs including Bankstown, Liverpool, Campbelltown, Penrith, Parramatta, Fairfield, Canterbury, Blacktown, and many more areas. Contact us to confirm we service your specific location.

Do you offer a warranty or guarantee?

Yes, all our pest control treatments come with a 3 months warranty. Our service covers ants, spiders, and cockroaches. If these pests return within the warranty period, we'll come back and re-treat the affected areas at no extra charge. We stand by our work with complete satisfaction guaranteed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is due upon completion of service. We provide invoices for all treatments and can arrange payment plans for ongoing maintenance contracts.
